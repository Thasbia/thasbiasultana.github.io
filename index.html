About me

Envision this: merging creativity with code, treating problem-solving as both an art and a science. My journey extends beyond conventional technical boundaries, encompassing a unique set of skills that transcend the standard cyber landscape. It's about intertwining creativity, strategic thinking, and technical proficiency.

Fancy Horizontal Timeline

Working in Retail

In 2017, I immersed myself in the realm of retail kickstarting my career in a dynamic and customer-centric industry. Throughout the years, I refined my skills in sales, customer service, and store operations, accumulating valuable experience that laid the foundation for my professional journey.

University

I chose to attend the University of Bradford to pursue occupational therapy, and I am proud to have graduated with first-class honors. The program, featured an integration of hands-on placements and rigorous theoretical studies, played a pivotal role in refining my skills and shaping my commitment to enhancing individuals' well-being.

Occupational Therapist

After earning my degree in occupational therapy from the University of Bradford. I started working as an occupational therapist,specialising in community-based occupational therapy. My responsibilities encompass facilitating individuals' engagement in various community settings, tailoring interventions to address unique needs, and promoting autonomy.

Paralegal

After deciding not to pursue a career in occupational therapy, I came across an opportunity to become a paralegal, drawn to the role's analytical and problem-solving aspects. However, I soon found that merely being a paralegal did not fully satisfy my professional aspirations, prompting me to explore avenues where I could leverage my skills for greater impact and personal fulfillment.

UA92 Bootcamp

Enrolling in the UA92 bootcamp in cloud engineering with Cybersecurity not only deepened my understanding of these fields but ignited a profound interest in cybersecurity. The experience opened my eyes to the diverse roles within the technology sector, it became a pivotal moment that motivated me to explore and pursue a career in this dynamic field.

THG Apprenticeship

Motivated by the insights gained during the UA92 bootcamp in cloud engineering with cybersecurity, I secured an apprenticeship with THG. Opting for an apprenticeship was driven by my eagerness for practical experience, ongoing learning, and mentorship,I believe this provides a holistic approach to building a strong foundation for my cybersecurity career.
